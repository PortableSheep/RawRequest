Why RawRequest

Everything you need, nothing you don't.

From chained variables to load tests, RawRequest keeps the powerful bits and trims the bloat.

Live cancellation

Each execution receives a unique ID so you can abort noisy chains the moment requirements change.

Environment aware

Scoped environments follow your .http file, ensuring the request running in prod is the one you meant.

Scriptable hooks

Use lightweight pre/post scripts to transform payloads, seed variables, or assert on responses.

Chained responses

Reference {{request1.response.body.id}} anywhere and RawRequest resolves it on the fly.

Load testing

Turn any request into a burst test with concurrency + iterations, then inspect the built-in metrics modal.

Offline friendly

No cloud account or sync needed. Everything lives in your repo, making diffs and reviews painless.

Editor-native flow

Compose ‚Üí Execute ‚Üí Inspect ‚Üí Automate

RawRequest reads your .http files, shows grouped requests, and queues executions so you never double-run by accident. When requirements shift, hit cancel and pick up right where you left off.
